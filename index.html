Welcome!

This is the official website of the Environment and Natural Resources Office (ENRO) under the Provincial Government of Romblon.

Vision

Prosperity through sustainable utilization, development and management of the environment and natural resources of Romblon.

Mission

To sustain economic development of the environment and natural resources of Romblon, promote responsible mining towards a socially, economically and environmentally stable and sustainable mineral industry using environment friendly technologies and pro-active people’s participation.

Our Commitment

Protecting Romblon's natural heritage for future generations.
