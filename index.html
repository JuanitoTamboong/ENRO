About ENRO Romblon

The Environment and Natural Resources Office (ENRO) of Romblon is dedicated to the protection, conservation, and sustainable management of the province's rich natural resources.

Established to implement environmental laws and programs at the local level, ENRO works tirelessly to ensure that Romblon's forests, coastal areas, and mineral resources are preserved for future generations while supporting sustainable development.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

Prosperity through sustainable utilization, development and management of the environment and natural resources of Romblon.

Mission

To sustain economic development of the environment and natural resources of Romblon, promote responsible mining towards a socially, economically and environmentally stable and sustainable mineral industry using environment friendly technologies and pro-active people's participation.

Our Commitment

Protecting Romblon's natural heritage for future generations
