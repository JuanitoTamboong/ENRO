About ENRO

Welcome to the official website of the Environment and Natural Resources Office (ENRO) under the Provincial Government of Romblon. We are dedicated to preserving and sustainably managing the natural resources of our beautiful province.

VISION

Prosperity through sustainable utilization, development and management of the environment and natural resources of Romblon.

MISSION

To sustain economic development of the environment and natural resources of Romblon, promote responsible mining towards a socially, economically and environmentally stable and sustainable mineral industry using environment friendly technologies and proactive people's participation.
